Presale

MinePro presalers will have the opportunity to participate with on timed presale system. First tier will be a discounted token price of 20%. When the first tier countdown is complete, each subsequent tier discount will drop to 10% then 5%. There are only 3 scheduled tiers!

NO PRESALE TOKENS WILL BE VESTED ON LAUNCH. You will receive 100% of your presale amount on TGE. If you choose to immediately enter the staking pool on TGE you will be granted a HUGE bonus reward multiplier for doing so!

Bonus Presale NFT’s

MinePro will be granting limited edition NFT’s to any investor contributing $500 or more!

Miner Joe NFT

Anyone who contributes $500 or more into the presale will receive a limited edition “Miner Joe” NFT. This NFT is the only way to earn an even HIGHER bonus multiplier on rewards, and grant access to the upcoming play to earn game being developed. That includes bonus rewards from the in game economy. NFT holders will be the only ones able to have this bonus multiplier and game access! (Picture of NFT will be provided)

Golden Joe NFT

Any presale contributor of $2000 or more will earn a limited edition “Golden Joe” NFT. This NFT has all the benefits of the Miner Joe NFT, but in addition, it unlocks an extra 2% airdrop allocation for SocialFi participants. That means any farming points earned during the SocialFi campaign are almost DOUBLED! (Picture of NFT will be provided)
